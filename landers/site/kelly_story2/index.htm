Kelly Story, LA

Kelly Story, LA is a Licensed Medical Aesthetician and Professional Laser Technician at Viva Day Spa + Med Spa. She provides advanced laser and cosmetic skin procedures at our downtown Austin med spa location, Viva Day Spa + Med Spa Lamar.

Caring for others has always been part of Kellys DNA. She began her career in the wellness industry as a licensed massage therapist in 1997. Her passion for healthy skin and helping others achieve their goals led her to enrolling at Avenue 5 Institute, where she received her aesthetic education. She joined Viva Day Spa as a licensed esthetician in 2017.

Kelly decided to further advance her skills and training when Viva expanded into med spa services in 2016. She attended the Texas Laser and Aesthetics Academy in 2019, specializing in Laser Hair Removal. She became part of Vivas medical spa team in 2018. Additionally, Kelly received advanced device training for Microneedling, RF Microneedling, Chemical Peels, HydraFacial, Dermaplaning, IPL Photofacial, and Body Contouring.

When shes not helping others look and feel their very best, shes pursuing her other passion travel. Kelly and her husband love hiking, especially with their dogs, and seeing the world. Theyre determined to reach their goal of someday visiting every Caribbean island.
