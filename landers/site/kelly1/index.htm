Kelly Clarkson’s Trouser Shorts Will Switch Up Your Basic Denim Rotation — Shop Lookalikes from $29

Get her look with styles from Amazon, Nordstrom, Madewell, and Spanx

By
Nicol Natale
Nicol Natale
Nicol Natale is a shopping writer at PEOPLE based in Hawai’i with more than six years’ experience in the digital and print media industry. She covers everything from the hottest celebrity fashion trends, including jeans, to the latest Roomba vacuum cleaner shoppers can’t stop buying from Amazon.
People Editorial Guidelines
Published on August 6, 2024 10:30AM EDT

Shorts are an obvious choice for your summer wardrobe. However, you may not always feel like wearing them in denim form. Well, Kelly Clarkson just showed us the comfiest alternative to jeans: trouser shorts.

In a recent post from The Kelly Clarkson Show, the singer and host wore a collection of celebrity-favorite pieces for summer, including a striped button-down blouse, open-toe shoes, and high-waisted trouser shorts. 

It’s not the first time Clakrson wore trouser shorts. Back in June, she wore the style in a summery floral print. For anyone looking for something much softer than denim, trouser shorts are both comfortable and elegant, so you can dress them up for dinners and events.

Want to give trouser shorts a try? Keep reading to pick up similar styles at top brands like Amazon, Spanx, Nordstrom, and more — they start at just $23.

Gap 365 High Rise Shorts

Orig. $60 $23 at Gap.com

If you want a pair of trouser shorts that look similar to Clarkson’s, this Gap pair is it. They have a high-rise waist, flattering pleats, and a belt loop to accessorize. The flowy silhouette is airy, making it a practical choice for summer. They’re currently 50 percent off in three staple colors: khaki, gray, and black.

Hotouch Bermuda Shorts

Orig. $33 $28 at Amazon

Bermuda shorts are a hot trend worn by Katie Holmes, Meghan Markle, and Mindy Kaling. These bermuda shorts come in a trouser style that combines two trends. The long length provides extra coverage, and the wide leg is super stylish. They’re available in 20 colors like camel, green, white, and pink. One five-star reviewer called them “flattering” and “comfortable.” 

Spanx Carefree Crepe Trouser Shorts

$138 at Spanx

Ditch zippers and buttons for these trouser shorts by Spanx. The perfect combination of stylish and comfortable, they are made with a washable crepe fabric that's lightweight and breathable. They have an inner satin lining that doesn’t cling, and the stretchy waistband moves comfortably with you. You can snag them in sizes XS through 3XL.
